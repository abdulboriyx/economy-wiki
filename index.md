## Theories
- [Division of Labour](wiki/theories/division-of-labour.md) — Specialisation increases productivity
- [Invisible Hand](wiki/theories/invisible-hand.md) — Self-interested actors produce social benefit via markets
- [Absolute Advantage](wiki/theories/absolute-advantage.md) — Nations specialise in goods they produce most efficiently (Smith)
- [Comparative Advantage](wiki/theories/comparative-advantage.md) — Nations specialise by opportunity cost, not absolute efficiency (Ricardo)
